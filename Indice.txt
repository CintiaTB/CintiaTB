📂 Development Index
1. 🛡️ City Compliance Monitor
Problem: Fines for obstructing public roads due to undetected traffic restrictions.

The Solution: A bot that scans municipal bulletins (PDF) and cross-references data with active sites using Fuzzy Matching.

Value: 100% elimination of fines and automation of 2 hours of daily review.

Technology: Python, pdfplumber, fuzzywuzzy.
 
2. 💰 Revenue Recovery Engine
Problem: Revenue loss due to unbilled immobilized assets (containers/machinery).

The Solution: Stock audit algorithm that automatically detects days of storage outside of contract.

Value: Direct recovery of lost revenue and optimization of asset turnover.

Technology: Python, Pandas, Data Analytics.

3. 👥 Cloud-First HR & Absence Portal
Problem: Chaotic management of personnel, vacations, and paper-based absence documents.

The Solution: Digital ecosystem for managing absences and scheduling in real time.

Value: Transition to a paperless model and complete visibility for the traffic department.

Technology: HTML5, Firebase (NoSQL), JavaScript.

4. 🚛 Fleet Preventive Maintenance System
Problem: Preventable breakdowns and lack of traceability in heavy fleet maintenance.

The Solution: Repair control app linked to the vehicle database for preventive alerts.

Value: Increased fleet lifespan and reduced vehicle downtime.

Technology: Firebase Realtime DB, HTML/JS.

5. 📄 Smart Proforma Generator
Problem: Complexity in calculating custom rates and excessive time spent generating invoices.

The Solution: Automated proforma generator with dynamic pricing logic.

Value: Reduced invoicing time and elimination of calculation errors.

Technology: Python, Jinja2 (Templates), PDF Generation.
